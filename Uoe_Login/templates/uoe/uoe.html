Our Facilities

The University of Eldoret offers a wide range of modern facilities
to enhance the learning and overall experience of its students.
The campus boasts state-of-the-art laboratories equipped with the latest technology,
providing students with hands-on experience in their respective fields of study. For students interested in sports and physical activities, the university provides excellent sporting facilities,
including well-maintained fields and courts for football, basketball, volleyball, and more.
These facilities encourage a healthy and active lifestyle among students, fostering teamwork and a sense of camaraderie. In addition, the University of Eldoret offers a well-stocked library that serves as a valuable resource for research and study.
With a vast collection of books, journals, and online databases, students have access to a wealth of information to support their academic pursuits. Furthermore, the university campus provides comfortable and modern residential facilities for students who prefer to live on-site.
The accommodation options are designed to create a conducive living environment,
promoting a sense of community and facilitating easy access to campus facilities.

OUR LIBRARY

Welcome to the University of Eldoret Library,
a hub of knowledge and learning that serves as the academic heartbeat of our institution.
Our library is a vibrant space dedicated to supporting the educational and research needs of our students, faculty, and staff.

ACCOMODATION

Our student residences are designed to cater to the diverse needs and preferences of our students.
Each room is furnished with essential amenities, including study desks,
comfortable beds, and storage spaces, ensuring a conducive environment for academic pursuits.
We prioritize safety and security, with dedicated security personnel and surveillance
systems in place to ensure the well-being of our residents.

PLAYGROUNDS

Our playgrounds offer a variety of amenities and facilities designed to cater to the diverse interests of our students.
From open fields for sports like football, rugby, and athletics, to courts for basketball,
volleyball, and tennis, there are plenty of options to engage in physical activities and team sports.

E-LEARNING

The University of Eldoret's e-learning platform provides students with the opportunity to access course materials,
participate in virtual lectures, and submit assignments at their own pace,
accommodating diverse learning styles and personal schedules.
This flexibility allows students to balance their studies with work, family commitments, or other responsibilities.
